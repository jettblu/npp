Pass energy around a circle with a sequence of moves.

## Moves

- **Hadouken:** 1 direction,
- **Tiger uppercut:** reverse direction
- **Sonic boom:** to anyone
- **Yoga flame:** skip 1
- **Schwajab:** reverse direction and skip 2
- **Down shift to 4 speed:** stalling move
